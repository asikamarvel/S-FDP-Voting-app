## How Voting Works

This competition uses social media engagement as votes. When you interact with our posts on any platform, your engagement is tracked and counted towards the vote tally.

### Key Points:
- **Each engagement = 1 Vote**
- Your votes are tracked automatically when you engage with designated competition posts
- Some platforms allow **follower validation** (your vote may count more if you're a verified follower)
- You can vote on multiple platforms to maximize your impact!

## Twitter/X Instructions

**Official Account:** [@disease_society](https://twitter.com/disease_society)

### How to Vote on Twitter:

#### Step 1: Follow Our Account
First, make sure you're following **@disease_society** to maximize your vote's value.

1. Go to [twitter.com/disease_society](https://twitter.com/disease_society)
2. Click the **Follow** button

#### Step 2: Find the Competition Tweet
Look for tweets marked with **#SFDPVoting** or the competition announcement pinned to our profile.

#### Step 3: Cast Your Vote by Retweeting
**RETWEET** the designated competition tweet to cast your vote!

1. Find the competition tweet
2. Click the **Retweet** icon (two arrows in a square)
3. Select **"Retweet"** (not Quote Tweet)

> **Note:** Your vote is validated if you're a follower of @disease_society. Non-followers' retweets are still counted but may not receive full validation credit.

### What Gets Tracked on Twitter:
| Action | Tracked? | Counts as Vote? |
|--------|----------|-----------------|
| Retweet | ✅ Yes | ✅ **YES - PRIMARY VOTE** |
| Like | ✅ Counted | ❌ No (display only) |
| Reply | ✅ Counted | ❌ No (display only) |

## YouTube Instructions

**Official Channel:** [Society for Disease Prevention](https://youtube.com/channel/UCQpcdv3QCSSZmMLqPaVpkkA)

### How to Vote on YouTube:

#### Step 1: Subscribe to Our Channel
Subscribe to the **Society for Disease Prevention** YouTube channel.

1. Go to our channel page
2. Click the red **Subscribe** button
3. Enable notifications (bell icon) to stay updated

#### Step 2: Find the Competition Video
Look for videos in the **SFDP Voting Competition** playlist or videos with "VOTING COMPETITION" in the title.

#### Step 3: Cast Your Vote by Commenting
**COMMENT** on the designated competition video to cast your vote!

1. Scroll down to the comments section
2. Write a meaningful comment (be positive and supportive!)
3. Click **Comment** to submit

### Comment Guidelines:
- Be respectful and relevant to health topics
- Avoid spam or single-character comments
- One meaningful comment per video is sufficient

### What Gets Tracked on YouTube:
| Action | Tracked? | Counts as Vote? |
|--------|----------|-----------------|
| Comment | ✅ Yes | ✅ **YES - PRIMARY VOTE** |
| Like | ✅ Counted | ❌ No (display only) |
| Views | ✅ Counted | ❌ No (display only) |

> **Note:** Due to YouTube API limitations, subscriber lists cannot be accessed. All commenters' votes are counted equally.

## Important Rules

### General Rules:
1. **One vote per action per platform** - Multiple retweets/comments on the same post don't multiply your votes
2. **Be authentic** - No fake accounts or bots allowed
3. **Stay positive** - Hateful or spam comments may be disqualified
4. **Spread the word** - Encourage friends and family to participate!

### Voting Period:
- Competition dates will be announced on each platform
- Votes are counted in real-time
- Results will be published on our official accounts

### Disqualification Criteria:
- Using bot accounts or automated systems
- Creating multiple fake accounts to vote
- Posting spam, hateful, or inappropriate content
- Attempting to manipulate the voting system

## FAQs

### Q: Can I vote on all four platforms?
**A:** Yes! You can vote on Twitter, YouTube, Instagram, AND Facebook. Each platform counts separately, so voting on all four maximizes your impact.

### Q: Does being a follower matter?
**A:** On Twitter, votes from verified followers receive additional validation credit. On other platforms, all votes are counted equally.

### Q: How do I know my vote was counted?
**A:** Your engagement appears on the post publicly. Our tracking system automatically syncs engagements from each platform.

### Q: Can I vote multiple times on the same post?
**A:** Each unique engagement counts as one vote. Multiple likes or retweets on the same post don't add extra votes.

### Q: What if I don't have all social media accounts?
**A:** Vote on whichever platforms you have! Every vote counts regardless of which platform you use.

### Q: When will results be announced?
**A:** Results will be posted on all four social media platforms after the competition ends. Follow us to stay updated!
